  1. Can I foreclose or prepay my Personal Loan?

     Yes, you can prepay the entire loan outstanding anytime during the loan tenure after 1 instalment, subject to payment of standard pre-closure charges of 5% + GST. No part prepayment is allowed. For prepayment of your Personal Loan, please visit the nearest EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank branch.
    We request you to carry the following documents to complete the process:
        • Request letter signed by the loan applicant     • PAN Card (if the mode of payment is cash and the amount is more than Rs 50,000)     • Payment can be made through cash, cheque or demand draft.
  2. What is the procedure to prepay my Personal Loan?

     For prepayment of your Personal Loan, please visit the nearest EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank branch.
    We request you to carry the following documents to complete the process:
        • Request letter signed by the loan applicant.
        • PAN Card (if the mode of payment is cash and the amount is more than Rs 50,000)
        • Payment can be made through cash, cheque or demand draft.
  3. How much time will it take for my loan to be approved for Personal Loan?

     Loans are approved within 72 hours of submission of complete documentation.
  4. How can I prepay my Personal Loan as I am unable to visit branch?

     In case you are unable to visit our branch for prepayment of your loan, you can send your representative along with an authority letter duly signed by the loan applicant, for payment.
    The co-applicant or third party visiting the branch for closing the loan needs to carry the documents mentioned below:
    • Main applicant Identification proof (Driving License or Passport or Voters card or PAN Card)
    • Authorisation letter from main applicant.
  5. How do I repay the Personal loan?

     You can repay the loan in Equated Monthly Instalments (EMIs).
    If you have an EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank Account, you can mandate us to directly debit the same through auto debit mandate or if you have an alternate bank account, then through NACH mandate.
  6. What is the loan amount that can be sanctioned?

     Depends upon the limit set by credit buyer.
    Based on your kind of business and your relationship with EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank, the final loan amount is decided.
  7. What are the Interest Rates for Personal Loan?

     Personal Loan interest rates are applied basis the defined pricing matrix followed and standardised by the bank as per customer’s profile.
    Personal Loan rates vary from 11.25% to 22.00% per annum.
  8. Do I have to pay any additional charges for Personal Loan?

     A processing fee is levied on loan, basis the defined pricing matrix followed by the bank.
    This fee will be deducted from the disbursal amount payable to you.
  9. Can I pre-close or pre-pay my Business Instalment Loan (BIL)?

     Yes, You can pre-close or pre-pay your Business Instalment Loan (BIL) by paying the applicable pre-closure charges after payment of one Equated Monthly Instalment (EMI).
    Currently, the applicable pre-closure charge is 5% of the outstanding loan amount (plus applicable tax).
  10. How can I approach EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank for Personal Loans?

     You can make a request for new Personal Loan through the following modes:

    Online process without login:
    Log in to www.European Creditbank.com > Products> Loans > Select Personal loan > Request a call.

    Internet banking:
    Log in to Internet Banking > My Accounts > Select Loans > Apply for Loans > Under Personal Loan> Click on Apply Now > Check the Eligibility and click on Apply Now.
    Log in to Internet Banking > Main Page >You can view the pre-qualified offers for Personal Loan > Click on Instant Approval.

    Customer Care:
    You may call our Customer Care on <18601207777> between 08:00 a.m. and 08:00 p.m. To know the Customer Care numbers, please click here.
    EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank branch : Please visit the nearest EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank Asset Servicing Branch along with your valid ID proof, address proof, 3 months bank statement in which salary is credited, latest 3 months salary slips and Form -16 for the current/ previous financial year.
  11. What are the required documents under each program?

     The documents required are KYC Documents + Income Documentation.
  12. Can my partner and I, as owners in our partnership firm, apply together for EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank Business Instalment Loan?

     Yes. EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank offers a facility where in both partners can together apply for EUROPEAN CREDIT Bank Business Instalment Loan.
